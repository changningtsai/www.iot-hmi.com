About Us

We live in a world whose meanings are built on communications. Communications facilitate the flow and exchange of information. The flow and exchange of information advances our civilization. Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (“IOT”) is founded with the vision to enhance the efficacy of the communications between humans, humans and machines, and machines. IOT’s technology helps our customers to increase productivity. IOT’s solutions bring the Human-Machine Interface (HMI), machine-to-machine communications and Edge-to-Cloud communications to the next level.
