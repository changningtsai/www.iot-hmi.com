Six seasoned entrepreneurs launched Immense Oak Technologies, Inc.

May 31, 2019

Hsinchu, Taiwan – Six innovative and seasoned entrepreneurs founded Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (IOT) to address the ever-increasing demand for high-performance Human-Machine Interface (HMI) and Machine-to-Machine (M2M) communication solutions.

The strategy of IOT is centered around how to enhance the communication efficacy between human and machine and between machines. The efficacy is the driving force for elevating productivity. IOT’s solution will help manage the information and facilitate the flow of the information. “IOT’s solution will bring the HMI to the next level,” said Anthony Guo, President of IOT, “In addition, we’ll build the edge computing/fog computing around the HMI for delivering the M2M and machine-to-Cloud communication.”

IOT’s solutions have the advantages of easy-to-adopt and support for expansion. The customers can tap into the vast amount of built-in resources to cut the time-to-market cycle time. The expansion capability allows customers to build products to address a wide array of applications. “IOT thrive to provide the solutions that can quickly turn customer’s idea to a final product,” said Rich Chuang, CTO of IOT. “Our technology is centered around the idea of helping our customers to achieve product development goals.”

IOT’s founders come from diverse engineering backgrounds. In addition to the engineering expertise, these founders have brought several successful products from development to production with stellar financial results. In founding IOT, we believe we can build a compony with a open-minded culture and friendly working environment that help talented people excel.

About IOT

Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. is a leading solution provider for high performance Human-Machine Interface. Our solutions facilitate effective and efficient communications between humans and machines and between machines. Headquartered in Hsinchu, Taiwan, IOT thrives to serve our customers globally. Additional information about IOT and IOT’s solutions can be found at www.iot-hmi.com.
